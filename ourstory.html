The Proposal

In the fall of 2009, Pat was just starting his freshman year at the University of Dayton, and Kiersten was a sophomore. We first met during rehearsals for The Lark, a theatre show we were both in. We immediately bonded over our similar taste in music, punny sense of humour, and love of theatre.

After spending time together at rehearsals, hanging out with mutual friends, and attending concerts, Kiersten started to develop a crush on Pat. Though we were very close, the timing wasn't right, and we stayed friends until 2 years later.

Our friendship continued to grow, especially during the summer of 2011 when we both stayed at UD. We had a lot of lunches together at the cafeteria and on Brown St., and spent many nights playing board games with friends.

One night, Pat invited Kiersten over to watch "Garden State". That was the beginning of our more than friendship. For the next few months, we started spending more and more one- on-one time together, until we finally decided that we should officially "date".

Kiersten graduated in May 2012, but stayed in Dayton during Pat's senior year. The good times continued, and after Pat's graduation in 2013 we moved into neighboring building in the same apartment complex. There we spent many a night binge-watching Netflix, drinking cheap wine, and testing out Pinterest recipes.

After watching an episode of Parks & Rec (April & Andy go to the Grand Canyon), we were inspired to take a spontaneous road trip! We drove all the way to Virginia Beach to see the ocean. After a fancy dinner at Aldo's Italian Restaurant, we took a long barefoot moon-lit walk on the beach. Once we were alone on the beach, Pat got down on one knee and pulled the ring out of his pocket. Somehow Kiersten managed to get a "yes" out through all the tears and laughter!
