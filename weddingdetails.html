The wedding ceremony will begin at 3:30pm at Journey Christian Church in Brownsburg, IN. The church is located off of E. US Highway 136, between Green St. and W. Northfield Dr. The address is 6690 E. U.S. Highway 136, Brownsburg, IN 46112.

Cocktail hour at the reception will start around 4:30ish, after the ceremony ends. Dinner and dancing will begin shortly after. Festivities will be held in the Avon Wedding Barn, and surrounding greenery. Please park at the Bread of Life Church next door. There will be a golf cart available for those who need assistance. The address is 7424 E. County Road 100 North Avon, IN 46123.

We have reserved a block of rooms at the Fairfield Inn & Suites in Avon for the night of Saturday, May 16. Just mention the Manifold/Lillis wedding and they'll mark you down for our block. May is race month in Indianapolis, so hotels fill fast. We strongly recommend you book early! There will be a shuttle running back and forth from the Fairfield to the barn for anyone staying there who doesn't want to drive to the reception. The address for the Fairfield is 119 Angelina Way Avon, IN 46123.
